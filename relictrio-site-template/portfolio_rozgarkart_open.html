FINDING WORK IS NOW EASY WORK WITH DIGNITY

Challenge

Blue-collar jobs are always on demand but connecting with the right business can be tough at times. The same goes for local enterprises who are in search of skilled workers.To solve this lack of communication and trust between the two parties, the app language and registration methods were two important onboarding pain points

Brief

With Rozgarkart, find thousands of blue-collar workers for your business to fill in both seasonal and permanent openings. Hire the right people for the right job, at the lowest possible costs! We select only qualified and reliable workers and provide you with your needed workforce quickly!
